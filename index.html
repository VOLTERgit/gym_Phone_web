Incline Dumbbell Press
Set the bench to 30–45°. Hold dumbbells at chest level, palms forward. Press up explosively, squeeze chest at top. Lower slowly (3 sec) for maximum stretch. Keep shoulder blades pinched back throughout.

Flat Machine Press
Adjust seat so handles are at chest height. Grip handles, retract scapula. Push forward until arms almost fully extended. Control the return — don't let the weight stack slam. Machine = consistent resistance curve, safer for beginners.

Pec Deck Fly

Lat Pulldown
Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders, overhand grip. Lean back slightly (15°), pull bar to upper chest. Lead with elbows, not hands. Squeeze lats hard at bottom. Slowly extend arms back up — feel the full lat stretch at the top.

Execution Rules
⏱️
Time Limit
60–75 min max per session. Energy conserve karo for your job. Quality over quantity.
😤
Rest Periods
60–90 seconds between sets. Enough recovery to maintain form, short enough to keep intensity.
🎯
Form First
Perfect form → injury avoid. Machine-based training reduces CNS fatigue for working professionals.
🚫
No Daily Failure
Avoid failure sets every session. Heavy legs only once a week. Smart training = better recovery.
